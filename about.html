The Alchemy of Olivia & Co.

An immersive journey through the sacred microclimates, ancestral soils, and the precise, oxygen-free cold-extraction that defines our award-winning liquid gold.

The Sanctuary & The Soil

Our estates are located in a rare geological microclimate in Santa Barbara, where cold Pacific morning sea breezes are trapped by the Santa Ynez mountain range. This creates a high diurnal temperature variation, forcing the olive trees to concentrate sugars and build intense, complex aromatic profiles.

The soil is a highly unique clay-loam interwoven with Miocene marine fossil beds. Our trees are nourished by complex underground mycorrhizal networks, drawing rich minerals that impart a subtle marine salinity and flinty mineral note to our finished oils.

Seasons of the Grove

Our orchards are a living, breathing polyculture. Here is how our estate transforms across the cycle of the year.

Spring

Whispering Blossom

Delicate white blossoms blanket the hills like snow. Our local honeybees begin their pollination dance amidst ancient heirloom branches, setting the stage for the tiny fruit nodes.

Summer

Solar Synthesis

Drinking in over 300 days of warm Californian sunlight, the olives expand rapidly, converting clean solar energy into valuable health-supporting polyphenols and rich, aromatic compounds.

Autumn

The Midnight Harvest

To preserve flavor volatiles, we harvest at night under powerful floodlights. Harvesting in cold temperatures prevents premature fermentation, keeping the fruit pristine.

Winter

Silent Regeneration

Wild mustard, clover, and vetch carpet the grove floors. These cover crops naturally fix nitrogen back into the soil and prevent run-off, closing our organic, regenerative agricultural loop.

The Seven Alchemical Stages of Extra Virginity

True Extra Virgin Olive Oil is a fragile, fresh fruit juice. To capture its soul and keep its acid content near zero, our extraction process is a race against time, executed in a completely sterile, cold environment.

I

The Midnight Pluck

Olives are hand-raked into small, aerated crates to prevent crushing. Night picking ensures that the delicate outer skin of the fruit remains cool and firm, preserving volatile esters.

II

The Double Purge

Within 60 minutes of picking, the olives are leaf-stripped and washed twice using pure, chilled mountain spring water. No chemicals or detergents ever touch our harvest.

III

Stone Granulation

Unlike metal hammer mills that shear cells and generate high heat, our granite millstones slowly crush the olives, forming a rich paste without heating or bruising the cellular structures.

IV

Nitrogen-Shielded

The olive paste is gently churned at exactly 22°C (71.6°F) for 35 minutes. To prevent any oxidation of the precious polyphenols, the entire process takes place under a sealed blanket of pure nitrogen gas.

V

Speed Centrifugal

A horizontal decanter centrifuge spins the paste at high speeds. This separates the heavy solids and fruit water from the pristine green oil purely through mechanical force, with absolutely zero added heat.

Our Heirloom Cultivar Library

We actively protect near-extinct, heirloom olive varieties. We nurture and press each cultivar separately, allowing their distinct flavor personalities to shine.

Spicy

Koroneiki

Known as the king of Greek olives, these tiny fruits produce a vibrant, emerald-green oil. It features intense notes of fresh green banana, wild chicory, and a sharp, peppery throat finish that signals a very high polyphenol content.

Polyphenols: 680 mg/kg
Acidity: 0.14%
Herbaceous

Spanish

The heritage cultivar of Andalusia, our Picual trees produce a highly stable, aromatic oil. It opens with aromas of freshly cut grass, green tomato vine, and fig leaf, ending with a clean, pleasant almond bitterness.

Polyphenols: 520 mg/kg
Acidity: 0.11%
Elegant

Frantoio

The classic olive of Tuscany, Frantoio offers a beautifully balanced, fruit-forward experience. It yields a smooth, golden oil with aromas of green apple, sweet artichoke, and a mild, tingling black pepper finish.

Polyphenols: 410 mg/kg
Acidity: 0.16%

The Custodians of the Table

Olivia & Co. was founded in 1982 by master tasters Clara and Marcus Vance. Dissatisfied with the industrial blending practices that dominate the olive oil industry, they set out to establish a transparent, estate-grown alternative.

"Extra virgin olive oil is the blood of the soil. It cannot be rushed, and it cannot be faked. Every vintage we blend is an invitation to sit at our family table and experience agricultural purity in its most delicious form."

Global Accolades

Our single-estate and reserve blends are audited yearly by international sensory panels. We are honored to have our oils repeatedly selected for Gold Awards at the NYIOOC (New York International Olive Oil Competition) and ranked in the top tiers of the Flos Olei guide.
